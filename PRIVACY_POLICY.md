# Privacy Policy — Starshard 79

**Last updated:** April 11, 2026

## Overview

Starshard 79 is a mobile and desktop arcade game developed by PalaceSoft. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

## Data We Collect

### Data stored on your device only

- High score
- Settings preferences (sound, touch controls, visual quality)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

### Google Play Games Services

If you use the leaderboard feature, Google Play Games Services may collect:

- Your Google Play Games player ID
- Leaderboard scores you submit

This data is handled entirely by Google according to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not have access to your Google account information.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- No location data
- No device identifiers or advertising IDs
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports

## Third-Party Services

The app uses only one third-party service:

- **Google Play Games Services** — for leaderboard functionality only. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Advertising

This app contains no advertisements.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
